UTM Climbs 28 Places to Rank 153rd Globally in QS WUR 2026

Dear [506],

It is with great pride that we share Universiti Teknologi Malaysia’s (UTM) latest achievement in the QS World University Rankings 2026:

UTM now stands at 153rd globally, rising 28 places from 2025 and 50 places since 2023, a significant milestone that reflects our sustained commitment to academic distinction, impactful research, and international collaboration.

For the record, this is also UTM’s highest and best performance in the QS rankings to date, placing the university close to the top 10% of the world’s universities and highlighting its growing global academic and research stature. UTM's improved standing is driven by strategic progress across several key dimensions amongst them are:

  • International Research Network: +112 places (now 3rd in Malaysia)
  • Citations per Faculty: +45 places (3rd in Malaysia)
  • Academic Reputation: +8 places
  • Faculty-Student Ratio: +28 places
  • International Student Diversity: Ranked 132nd globally, representing 85 nationalities

With this latest achievement, UTM continues to be positioned among Malaysia’s top five Research Universities, advancing national priorities and contributing meaningfully to the global knowledge landscape.

Our Vice-Chancellor, Professor Dr. Mohd Shafry Mohd Rahim, remarked that this achievement underscores UTM’s evolution as a research-intensive and innovation-led institution, firmly rooted in a mission to humanise and democratise knowledge.

Guided by the values of “Menginovasi Penyelesaian Lestari” (Innovating Sustainable Solutions) and our motto “Kerana Tuhan untuk Manusia”, we strive to ensure that science and technology remain in service to society and humanity.

We extend our heartfelt appreciation to our alumni, academic collaborators, industry partners, and global allies. Your continued support and shared belief in our vision have been integral to this success.

Warm regards,
