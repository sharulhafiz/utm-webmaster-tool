Wishing You a Happy New Year and Celebrating UTM’s 2024 Highlights

 
Dear [388],

As we bid farewell to 2024 and welcome the new year, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) would like to express our highest appreciation for your constant support and cooperation. 2024 has been an exceptional year for us with multiple milestones achieved thanks to the trust and partnership of our outstanding collaborators. It is through this trust that we have advanced education, research and community engagement, innovating for the benefit of mankind. Hence, we’re excited to share some of UTM’s achievements in 2024:
  • Pioneering in AI: We proudly launched the nation’s first Faculty of Artificial Intelligence (FAI), cementing our position at the forefront of technological innovation.
  • Celebrating Our Graduates: This year, 6,829 undergraduate and postgraduate students graduated, joining our esteemed network of 232,222 alumni worldwide.
  • Leading in Employment: UTM achieved a 100% Graduate Employment (GE) rate, as reported by the Ministry of Higher Education’s Graduate Tracer Study 2023. This distinction places UTM first in the nation for producing highly skilled graduates with premium earning potential.
  • Global Recognition for Researchers: An impressive 51 UTM researchers were named among the World’s Top Scientists 2024 by Elsevier BV and Stanford University.
  • Climbing World Rankings: UTM is now ranked #181 globally in the QS World University Rankings 2025 and #28 in Asia in the QS Asia University Rankings 2025.
  • Excellence in Research: The Institute for Sustainable Transport (IST) was designated a Higher Education Centre of Excellence (HICoE), underscoring the impact of our research initiatives.
  • New Interdisciplinary Achievement: UTM ranked 1st in Malaysia and 35th globally out of 749 universities in the inaugural Times Higher Education (THE) Interdisciplinary Science Rankings (ISR) 2025.
  • Empowering Students: Our Royal Gala Dinner raised RM6.4 million, funding scholarships and financial aid for deserving students.
  • Global Exposure: 1,572 UTM students participated in outbound mobility programs across 23 countries, enriching their global perspectives through international attachments and internships.
As we step into 2025, we are eager to continue this remarkable journey with you. From all of us at UTM, we wish you and your loved ones a joyful, prosperous, and fulfilling new year. Thank you for being such an integral part of UTM’s story.

Innovating Solutions | In the Name of God for Mankind
